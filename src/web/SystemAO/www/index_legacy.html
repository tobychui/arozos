You are seeing this page instead of the login page because you have enabled home page mode

The ArozOS Home Page is a good way to show something simple for your organization or company when hosting on the cloud or a DIY NAS.

To edit this page, take a look at ./web/www/index.html

How can I turn it this off?

Remove the -enable_homepage flag from your startup script and restart. All users that are not logged in will be redirected to login page instead.
