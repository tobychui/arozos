About Chatspace

Chatspace is a team chat WebApp built on the ArozOS shared space collaboration backbone. Channels and direct messages are shared spaces; messages, threads, reactions and pins travel as space items in realtime over the space WebSocket channel.

Formatting: *bold*, _italic_, ~strike~, `code`, ```code block```, > quote, - bulleted lists, :thumbsup: icon shortcodes and @username mentions.

Shortcuts: Ctrl+K jump to a conversation, Enter to send, Shift+Enter for a new line, Up arrow to edit your last message.
